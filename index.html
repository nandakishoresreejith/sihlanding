About Water Saathi

Water Saathi is an initiative to promote public participation in groundwater conservation. Our platform empowers individuals and communities to easily estimate the feasibility and potential of rooftop rainwater harvesting at their locations. We provide personalized information and guidance to help build a sustainable water future.
